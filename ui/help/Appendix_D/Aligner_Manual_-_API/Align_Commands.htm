Align Commands

APF

Loads the wafer to the chuck, performs a measurement cycle, aligns the wafer’s primary fiducial (notch or flat) to the angle specified by the SOA command, and returns when all motion is complete.

ALN

Loads the wafer to the chuck, performs a measurement cycle, aligns the wafer’s notch or flat to the specified SOA angle, turns off the vacuum chuck, and raises the lifting pins, and returns when all motion is complete.

RWP

Loads the wafer to the chuck, performs a measurement cycle and aligns the wafer’s primary fiducial (notch) to the angle specified by the SOA command. Returned angles are in millidegrees from 0 to 359999 measured CW from the sensor as viewed from above. Return will not be sent until all motion is complete or there is a failure.

 

Returns the following values:

<s32 bit> Radial eccentricity in microns

<s32 bit> Eccentricity vector angle in millidegrees (0 to 359999)

<s32 bit> Primary fiducial angle in millidegrees (0 to 359999)

SOA<s32bit>

Sets the offset angle (measured CW from the sensor looking down from above the aligner) that the notch or flat will be centered at after the APF, ALN, or RWP command.
