Electrical Hazards

The IR-820 robot makes use of potentially lethal voltages. During normal operations and maintenance, there is adequate protection for the user from these hazards. However, if you remove the robot's protective chassis cover, or open the controller, you will need to take special precautions.

 

The robot chassis cover carries stickers cautioning you about electrical hazards inside the robot.

 

WARNING: Never reach into the controller while it is plugged into the electrical main, even if the controller is powered off. Always unplug the power cable at the controller prior to performing any maintenance inside the controller.

 

WARNING: NEVER reach into the robot chassis while the robot is powered. Always ensure that the controller is turned off and the cables to the robot are unplugged prior to perming any maintenance inside the robot.
