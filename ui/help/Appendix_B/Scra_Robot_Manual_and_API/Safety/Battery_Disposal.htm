Battery Disposal

The IR-820 robot uses four "C" cell alkaline batteries for encoder backup, while the IR-820C controller uses a coin-type lithium battery for low-level system setting backup.

 

The "C" batteries should be replaced after the robot is first powered up to ensure maximum lifetime, see Chapter 6(Unpacking and Installation).

 

Under normal operations, the controller's lithium battery should not need to be replaced.

 

Before replacing any batteries, and before retiring the controller at its end of life, contact your local waste management company for information on proper disposing of these batteries in your area.
