Inspection of System Equipment and Maintenance of consumables

It is dangerous to be within the operating space and perimeter of the robot arm during operation, and adequate safety precautions must be observed. The following are some general safety precautions and considerations to ensure operator safety:

 

WARNING: Do not operate the robot under automatic control while within the robot work area.

 

WARNING: Except when specifically necessary, turn off power to the controller while carrying out maintenance. Lock the power switch, if necessary, so that no other person can turn it on. The robot chassis cover carries stickers cautioning you about mechanical hazards inside the robot.

 

WARNING: Disable power to the servo motors during access. Even when the robot is stationary, it may be in a "ready to move" state and waiting for a signal. In this state, the robot should be regarded as still in motion.

 

CAUTION: If teach pendant controls are to be used, test the motion controls, EMERGENCY STOP, and LIVEMAN switches to assure their proper operation.

 

CAUTION: When replacing parts, be sure to use those parts specified by IRSI. In particular, always use fuses or other parts of the specified ratings. Using improper fuses or parts may cause a fire or result in component damage.
