Physical Installation Safety

IRSI advises at least two people install and handle the robot. The IR-820 robot with 12.9" vertical travel can weigh as much as 80 pounds. Attempting to lift a robot in and out of your system without any assistance can cause bodily injury.

 

CAUTION: Only lift the robot by the upper flange, the body, or underneath the body. Never lift the robot by its arm.

 

CAUTION: Do not let your fingers come between the robot flange and the mounting surface.

 

The top of the robot case carries a sticker cautioning you of the weight of the robot. During installation, pay particular attention to the robot arm links. When the robot is not powered, or the servos are not active (de-servoed), moderate pressure can move the arm links. The spaces between the two arm link and the end effector present pinching hazards.

 

CAUTION: Do not close your hand around an arm link or the end effector. When moving the robot arm manually, always do so with an open hand, moving the upper arm link only.

 

The robot carries stickers cautioning you about the robot's pinch points.
