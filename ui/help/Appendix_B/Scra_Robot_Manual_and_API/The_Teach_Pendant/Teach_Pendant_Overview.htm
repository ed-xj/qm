Teach Pendant Overview

The teach pendant is a hand-held terminal used for communicating with the robot controller. Use of the teach pendant is not mandatory, as most functions available on the teach pendant are also available using the host interface. The primary exceptions to this are jogging and near real-time position display.

 

Two types of teach pendant are available: one with a liveman switch, and one without. The two teach pendant types are otherwise identical.

 

The teach pendant's primary features are:

  1. The display.

  2. The keypad.

  3. The emergency stop, or E-stop switch

  4. Optionally, a liveman switch.

 

The E-stop and liveman switches are connected directly to controller's power control circuitry. Any time the teach pendant is plugged into the controller, these switches are enabled. Unplugging the teach pendant from the controller will result in an emergency stop to ensure safety in the event that the teach pendant becomes unplugged inadvertently.
