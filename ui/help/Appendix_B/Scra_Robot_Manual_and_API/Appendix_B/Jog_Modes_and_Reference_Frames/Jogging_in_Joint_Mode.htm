Jogging in Joint Mode

In joint mode, the IR-820's four axes (Z, shoulder, elbow, and wrist) move independently.

 

To put the robot into Joint jog mode, press the "Jog Mode" button until JOINT is displayed on the teach pendant. In JOINT mode, the jog keys act as follows:

 

Button Action Direction

Z-/Z+ Jogs the robot arm up and down (Z) + is up

X/R-/+

Shoulder

Rotates the shoulder + is counterclockwise

Y/

Theta -/+

Elbow

Rotates the elbow + is counterclockwise

Wrist 1 -/+ Rotates the wrist + is counterclockwise

 

Note that the individual robot arm links do not change their orientation when a single joint is jogged. This is very different than how an R-Theta robot works, and until you become familiar with the motion of the robot, go slowly and pay attention.
