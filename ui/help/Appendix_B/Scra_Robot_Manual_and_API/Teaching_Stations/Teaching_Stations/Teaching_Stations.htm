Teaching Stations

Creating a robot station consists of the following steps:

  1. Moving the robot to the desired position.

  2. Issuing the "Learn" command.

  3. Setting station parameters.

  4. Saving the station database.

 

A station consists of both a "Perch" and an "Inside" position. The "Perch" position is the position that is safely outside a wafer cassette or tool chuck, and may be used as a resting position prior to going into the cassette or chuck. The "Inside" position is the position at which the wafer will be picked up or dropped off.

 

The inside position may not be used, as in the case of a station used only between other stations as a safe waypoint (also called a "via"), but a perch position is required for each station.

 

In the case of a wafer pick-up or drop-off station, you teach both the inside and perch positions.
