Placing the Robot in the Work Cell

Set the robot on the floor or in a support for preliminary setup. DO NOT HOOK UP ANY CABLES AT THIS TIME.

 

  1. For EACH joint (fore arm, aft arm, wrist) perform the following steps:

    • Slowly rotate the joint counter clockwise by hand until a hard stop is reached.

    • Slowly rotate the joint back clockwise approximately 320 degrees, so that the bottom arm is pointing away from the battery-box opening, the top arm (the "forearm") is pointing toward it, and the end-effector mounting point on the wrist is pointing away.

 

The arm should now be in a "folded-up" position. The robot can now be installed in the work area.

 

  1. Place the robot in its mounting hole, and secure it with three bolts through the mounting holes in the robot flange.

 

CAUTION: Do not lift the robot by its arm. Lift the robot only by the body or the mounting ring.

 

CAUTION: Do not attempt to lift the robot by yourself. The unit can weight over eighty pounds.
