Inspection of System Equipment and Maintenance of Consumables

It is dangerous to be within the operating space and perimeter of any robotic components during operation, and adequate safety precautions must be observed. The following are some general safety precautions and considerations to ensure operator safety.

 

WARNING: Do not operate any of the robotic components under automatic control while within the tool work area.

 

WARNING: Except when specifically necessary, turn off power to the tool while carrying out maintenance.
